How it works

  • First step

    Create a check list of items of products you buy frequently. make sure to add the products in the order of consumption. you will be able to see your list once you are finished creating your check list.

  • Second Step

    Once you have possession of you item listed on your checklist. Click on them and add the expiration date for that particular item. The website will automatically set notification's to you for a prior time that you decide. When you receive your notification you can offer it up or finish.

  • Final Step

    Once a notification has been received. You can allow it to be announced this mean everyone in your house will know to start consuming this product. It will also let your family know if your are posting it for trade. This way you can exchange items if you fill like you no longer what the item that is about to expire.
